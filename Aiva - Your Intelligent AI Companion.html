How to Use Aiva

1

Power On

Press and hold the power button for 5 seconds to turn on your Aiva. You'll see a startup animation on the screen.

2

Initial Setup

On first boot, Aiva will enter Bluetooth setup mode. Connect your phone to Aiva via Bluetooth and provide your WiFi credentials through the simple setup process.

3

Voice Activation

Say "Hello Aiva" or press the physical button to activate listening mode. Aiva will show a listening animation and wait for your command.

4

Ask Anything

Ask questions, request music, set reminders, or just chat. Aiva will process your request and respond intelligently.

5

Charging

When the battery is low, connect the USB Type-C cable to charge. Aiva will show a charging animation on the screen.
